About us

The wydevelopment team is trying to improve wydevices functionality.

Feel free to join us at the wydevelopement forums at http://foro.wydev.es


You can also create and manage new requests at the wydevelopment collaboration portal. Join us

Follow us on your wydevice adding us on your webservices, you can check instructions here

Basically we will need help improving the devices.
We're currently searching people with this type of profile:

Requestor: Anonymous (or not) user which has the idea, they request it and provide information about the concept

WyProject Manager: Registered and involved, he should review request and if he find any interesting, and he(she) thinks will be able to break into tasks pieces to delegate will create a new project. This new project should have an ammount of tasks identified by which kind of knowledge is needed

WyDeveloper: A wydeveloper is a highly skilled programmer that can understand and make the job. In order to don't have a lot work to do, he will be assuming some tasks identified by a project manager and do it. Will be nice to record the process with GTKRecordMyDesktop. He will not need to edit and publish the video, just record the session when doing it and upload to a shared space (For example dropbox) other users can process the video, add the language and even subtitles! there's no need to expend developer's time he will just investigate and share the way a thing is done.

WyTester: Anybody who is just a owner and just want to help, there's no need to have any knowledge to follow video instructions or provide feedbacks.

WyDesigner: A skilled designer, or people who shares interest on design. For example, if a project will be handling the GUI, a task could be to improve icons. Maybe a wydesigner is creating the icons following some specification from a wyproject manager, 30x30, 16 colors ... i don't know but there are very highly skilled people out there on this area!

WyContent Redactor: A WyContent redactor is a person who has knowledge of writing and is comfortable doing it, It has medium skills on computers and is able to following instructions from the wyproject manager and notes & video from the wydeveloper in order to write articles about it on his own language. It's very important, because it will free the wydevelopers of explaining the details about the trick to main public. In the case some doubt also they can ask the wydevelopers.

WyContent Translator: Spread the word. The only skill needed to do this task is know some languages. I think in a open world, and the language is a problem for european people. I will be very pleased if we're able to collaborate each other in our original language, but, if we can also add translation to other languages we will be able to spread the word. if you know more language than your own, please, add yourself to this and translate content's already added ;-)

WyVideo Editor: People who is interested on video edition, they will edit the footage provided by the wydeveloper, ask for new needs to wyDesigner in order to add materials to footage, and finally will compose the video to use on the podcast's section.

Spread the word!
